About Biogas

Biogas is a renewable energy source produced through anaerobic digestion of organic waste like cow dung (CD) and kitchen waste (KW). It provides affordable cooking gas, reduces greenhouse emissions, and produces bio-slurry for fertilizer.

How to Build a Simple Digester

Step 1: Gather Materials

25L plastic gallon, PVC pipes, tyre tube, gas valve, glue gun, silicon seal, epoxy, cow dung, kitchen waste, and water.

Step 2: Prepare Feedstock

Chop kitchen waste, mix with cow dung in ratios (1:1, 1:2, 1:3), dilute with water to form slurry.

Step 3: Assemble Digester

Fix gas outlet hose to tyre tube for storage. Seal joints tightly with glue/epoxy. Provide a slurry outlet pipe.

Step 4: Collect & Use Gas

Store at ambient temperature (24–30°C). Measure gas with water displacement or tube expansion. Use safely for cooking or heating.
